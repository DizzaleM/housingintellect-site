The Problem

A $4.7B market
running on spreadsheets
and email.

The tools affordable housing compliance professionals use every day have not meaningfully evolved in decades. The stakes have never been higher.

“When a property loses its tax credit status, residents lose their homes. Compliance is not a back-office function — it is the difference between a family having a roof and not having one.”
01

Dangerously Outdated Tools

Annual compliance reports still submitted via Excel spreadsheets to a single email address. Yardi and AppFolio were built for rent collection — not compliance intelligence.

02

Expertise Walking Out the Door

Senior compliance professionals retire every day. Their institutional knowledge walks out with them. No system has existed to capture, scale, or replace it. Until now.

03

Catastrophic Stakes

A single compliance failure can trigger tax credit recapture — costing millions and displacing hundreds of families. The tools being used are inadequate for what is at risk.

04

Zero Portfolio Visibility

Compliance directors managing dozens of properties have no real-time view of risk. Problems surface at monitoring — by then, it is already too late.

Meet Aria

Your optional AI
compliance analyst.

Affordable housing compliance lives in grey areas. Aria knows that. She is not here to replace your judgment — she is here to support it when you want a second set of eyes, and step aside when you don’t.

Aria is always optional — your expertise always leads
Every Aria suggestion is a recommendation, not a mandate. Compliance requires professional judgment, and HousingIntellect is built to support that judgment — not override it. Aria flags. You decide. Always.
