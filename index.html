The Problem

A $4.7B market
running on spreadsheets.

The tools affordable housing compliance professionals rely on every day have not meaningfully evolved in decades. The stakes have never been higher.

"When a property loses its tax credit status, residents lose their homes. Compliance is not a back-office function. It is the difference between a family having a roof and not having one."
01

Dangerously Outdated Tools

Annual compliance reports submitted via Excel spreadsheets to a single email address. Yardi and AppFolio were built for rent collection, not compliance intelligence.

02

Expertise Walking Out the Door

Senior compliance professionals retire every day. Their institutional knowledge walks out with them. No system has captured, scaled, or replaced it. Until now.

03

Catastrophic Stakes

A single compliance failure can trigger tax credit recapture, displacing hundreds of families and costing millions. The tools being used are inadequate for what is at risk.

04

No Portfolio Visibility

Compliance directors managing dozens of properties have no real-time view of risk across their portfolio. Problems surface at monitoring. By then it is already too late.

Compliance Review Assistant

Meet Aria, your built-in
compliance review assistant.

Affordable housing compliance lives in grey areas. Aria knows that. She is not here to replace your judgment. She is here when you want a second set of eyes, and steps back when you do not.

Aria is always optional. Your expertise always leads.
Every Aria suggestion is a recommendation, not a mandate. Compliance requires professional judgment, and HousingIntellect is built to support that judgment. Aria flags. You decide. Always.

File Review Support

Optional

Ask Aria to review a file and she surfaces potential issues with regulatory citations. Or handle it yourself. Your call.

Income Calculation Check

Optional

Tips, self-employment, assets. Aria surfaces discrepancies. You verify and make the final determination.

Program Compliance Flags

Optional

VAWA, EIV, student rules. Aria watches for these when you want her to. You remain in full control.

Explainable Reasoning

Aria always explains why she flagged something. Agree, disagree, or override. Her output is a starting point, not a conclusion.
